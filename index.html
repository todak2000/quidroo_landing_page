About us

Quidroo is an innovative technology company that helps
SMEs finance their A/R and invoices to unlock their cash
flows and fund their growth and short term cash flow
problems. Our online platform allows the connection of
invoice and A/R sellers to a network of buyers and investors.
Out platform offers a fast, less expensive, secure and
automated environment that our team has built that uses a
patented in-house, incentive driven credit rating platform
that includes both public and privately derived company
data.
Quidroo opens its business to African SMEs and provides an
alternative, innovative solution to help reach their maximum
business potential and meet unanswered business financing
needs. We endeavor to provide a platform where the quality
of service is backed by a strong framework that makes the
invoice funding process less restrictive and produces more
consumer satisfaction and growth.
